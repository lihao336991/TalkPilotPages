🙋 Frequently Asked Questions

What is TalkPilot?

TalkPilot is an AI language conversation assistant for real-world conversations. It provides real-time translation, expression suggestions, native-language SOS help, and post-conversation reviews.

Which languages does TalkPilot support?

TalkPilot supports learning across multiple language pairs. The app works best when you set your native language and the language you want to practice, and it adapts its suggestions accordingly.

Does TalkPilot require an internet connection?

Yes, TalkPilot uses cloud-based AI models for speech recognition and suggestion generation, so an active internet connection is required during conversations.

Is my conversation data private?

We take privacy seriously. Conversation audio is processed in real time and is not stored permanently on our servers. Transcripts are saved locally on your device for review purposes. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID or Google account. Go to your device Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → TalkPilot to manage or cancel.

How does SOS mode work?

When you get stuck mid-conversation, activate SOS mode and speak in your native language. TalkPilot translates what you said into the target language and reads it aloud so the conversation keeps going.
